Play the Game of Life

Rules and Tricks

Posted by Antony Okwako on December 29, 2023

Life's a game, not a battle.

Florence Scovel Shinn in her classic writing; The Game of Life & How to Play It, we realize that there are secrets to winning this crafty game called life. The three spheres of the human mind: the subconcious, the conscious and the superconcious, prove to be a lifehack for everyone willing to tap into the magic power of these departments.

So put on your jersey and follow me on this interesting journey.

Building your Foundation - Know yourself.

As much as we have know-how on various topics and details about life and general stuff, we might be missing out on the core aspect of awareness which is self-awareness. Knowledge of our strengths, weaknesses, passions, likes, dislikes and our overal character and emotions is key in leading a fulfilling life. Practice self-evaluation and meditation to discover oneself while maintaining a stabe and calm equilibrium. Another important tip in discovering oneself is through journaling.

Cultivate a Positive Mindset

Focus on the positive aspects of life, even during difficult times. A positive mindset can improve your mental well-being and help you overcome obstacles more effectively. Focusing on the best that life has to offer has a great influence on one's attitude, character and zeal in pursuing one's own goals and dreams

Benefits of Positive Thinking

  • Reduced risk or symptoms of depression
  • Better psychological and physical wellbeing
  • Better communication and relationships with others
  • A greater sense of purpose in life
  • Reduced risk of cardiovascular disease
  • More happiness, fulfilment and meaning in your life

Tips on how to foster a positive mindset

Build and Maintain Relationships

Building and maintaining relationships is a cornerstone of a fulfilling and harmonious life. The connections we form with others significantly impact our well-being and contribute to personal growth. Whether they are personal or professional, investing in building and maintaining connections enriches our lives, providing support, understanding, and shared moments of joy.

Set Clear Goals

Setting clear goals involves defining specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) objectives that provide a roadmap for personal or organizational success. Clear goals provide direction, motivation, and a framework for decision-making.

Embrace Continuous Learning

Embracing continuous learning is a mindset that propels personal and professional growth. In a world that evolves rapidly, staying curious and committed to learning new things is not just beneficial; it's essential. embracing continuous learning is not just about acquiring knowledge; it's about adopting a mindset that propels you forward. It's a commitment to growth, adaptability, and a fulfilling life enriched by curiosity and a thirst for knowledge.

Prioritize your Health

Making your health a priority is not a selfish act but a responsible and essential step toward leading a more fulfilling and balanced life. By taking care of your body and mind, you're better equipped to handle life's challenges and savor its joys. Remember, your health is the foundation upon which you build your life's experiences, and investing in it pays dividends in the long run.

Practice Gratitude

Cultivating gratitude is an ongoing process, and it takes time to develop as a habit. However, Cultivating gratitude in life can have numerous positive effects on your mental and emotional well-being. Some of the ways you could cultivate and practice gratitude is by emracing the following:


  • Keep a Gratitude Journal.
  • Focus on the Positive.
  • Celebrate Small Wins.
  • Express Your Gratitude.
  • Volunteer or Help Others.

The art of navigating successfully lies in the ability to adapt, learn, and find meaning in every twist and turn. Embracing a growth mindset, cultivating resilience, and fostering gratitude can serve as valuable compass points, guiding you through the highs and lows. Life's journey is not about avoiding obstacles but about transforming them into stepping stones toward personal growth and fulfillment.
